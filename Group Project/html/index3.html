Grilled Chicken with Asparagus

Method:

  • 1. Place chicken in large resealable plastic bag. Add 1/4 cup of the marinade.

  • 2. Remove chicken and asparagus from marinade. Discard chicken marinade.

  • 3. Grill chicken over medium heat 6 to 7 minutes,Serve chicken with asparagus. Garnish with lemon slices.
